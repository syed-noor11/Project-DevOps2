Explore Northern Pakistan

Gilgit-Baltistan

Hunza

Beautiful valleys, welcoming people, and historic sites like Altit Fort and Baltit Fort.

  • Major Attractions: Attabad Lake, Passu Cones
  • Best Time to Visit: May – October
  • Local Food: Chapshuro, Diram Fitti
  • Cost Estimate: PKR 25,000 – 35,000 (3 days)

Skardu

Adventure hub with cold deserts and lakes surrounded by mountains.

  • Major Attractions: Shangrila Lake, Shigar Fort
  • Best Time to Visit: June – September
  • Local Food: Mamtu, Balti Soup
  • Cost Estimate: PKR 30,000 – 45,000 (4 days)

Fairy Meadows

One of the most picturesque spots in Pakistan, perfect for camping and hiking.

  • Major Attractions: Nanga Parbat View, Forest Trails
  • Best Time to Visit: June – August
  • Local Food: Traditional camp meals
  • Cost Estimate: PKR 20,000 – 30,000 (2 days)

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Swat

Known as the “Switzerland of Pakistan” with its lush green valleys and river.

  • Major Attractions: Fizagat Park, Malam Jabba
  • Best Time to Visit: April – October
  • Local Food: Chapli Kebab, Lamb Karahi
  • Cost Estimate: PKR 18,000 – 25,000 (3 days)

Kalam & Malam Jabba

Ski resort in Malam Jabba and pine-covered hills of Kalam are tourist favorites.

  • Major Attractions: Skiing, Ushu Forest, Blue Water
  • Best Time to Visit: December – February (Snow Season)
  • Cost Estimate: PKR 25,000 – 32,000 (2–3 days)

Naran & Kaghan

Famous for alpine lakes, glaciers, and scenic routes.

  • Major Attractions: Saif-ul-Malook, Babusar Top
  • Best Time to Visit: June – September
  • Local Food: Trout Fish, Sajji
  • Cost Estimate: PKR 20,000 – 28,000 (3 days)
