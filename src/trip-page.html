How to Travel

  • 🚌 Public buses (Daewoo, NATCO) connect major cities to Gilgit & Swat.
  • 🚙 Jeep rides are best for mountainous routes and inner valleys.
  • 👨‍✈️ Hiring local guides is recommended for remote treks.

Where to Stay

  • 🏨 Hotels in Skardu and Hunza (3-star and family-run options)
  • 🏡 Guest houses and homestays available in remote towns
  • 📱 Book via apps (Jovago, Sastaticket, etc.) or walk-in

Budgeting Tips

  • 🪙 Group travel reduces per-person cost
  • 🍽️ Eat local food and avoid chain restaurants
  • 📸 Some attractions charge entry — keep small cash handy

Packing Essentials

  • 🧥 Warm clothing (even in summer — nights are cold)
  • 🥾 Trekking shoes and waterproof gear
  • 🧴 Sunscreen, flashlight, reusable water bottle

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Wi-Fi available in northern areas?
Mostly in hotels and towns. Not in remote valleys.

Do I need a guide for all destinations?
Guides are optional in cities, but necessary for treks and rural regions.

What is the best travel season?
May to September for greenery, December to February for snow.
